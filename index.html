Places To Visit In Nepal

Swoyambhu Mahachaitya


Swayambhu is an ancient religious complex atop a hill in the
Kathmandu Valley, west of Kathmandu city. The Tibetan
name for the site means 'Sublime Trees'.

Pashupatinath Temple


Pashupatinath Mandir is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lord
Pashupati, and is located in Kathmandu, Nepal
